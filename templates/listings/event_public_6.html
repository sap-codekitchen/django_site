CodeKitchen Party

Come talk about anything

CodeKitchen is finishing up a semester of workshops covering the basics of programming, websites, and creating visualizations from APIs. We will be celebrating with snacks at our usual time and place.

Please feel free to stop by to talk about the future of CodeKitchen, suggestions for workshops, or is you just want to talk about some JavaScript you've been working on, random CSS things, your web portfolio, ideas for the Open Data Discourse challenge, or initiatives to gather and document data on racial discrimination and law enforcement accountability in the wake of Ferguson.

Prerequisites for this event: A vague interest in CodeKitchen or the role of technology in design and governance. And a desire for snacks.
