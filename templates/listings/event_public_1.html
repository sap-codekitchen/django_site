What is an API?

Getting geolocated photos from Instagram

The amazing Wenfei Xu will teach a workshop on how to get photos from Instagram based on their location. This involves learning about the Instagram API and how APIs work.

If you are interested in using social media for research, privacy implications of social media, or using media to tell stories about places, this workshop will give you a lot to work with.

As usual, there will be snacks. We have reserved an additional room for people who want to work on their own projects or tutorials.
